Help

Dashboard:

Once you login, you are redirected to your dashboard. Each user's dashboard is individual to that user. Here you can see all of your active bookings. You can view, the drone's number, the drone's origin, the drone's destination and the scheduled time of arrival.

Book:

The book menu allows you to book drones for travel. You are presented with a form. Once this form is filled, your drone is booked. In case, a drone is not available you are informed.

Information:

In the information menu, you can view the status of each drone. You can view where a drone currently is and if it already has a job. You can also view it's destination and scheduled time of arrival.
